Instructions

  1. 1.Make a dough from 250g flour, 125g butter, 1 egg, and a pinch of salt, then press it into a tart form and refrigerate.
  2. 2.2. Stir 300g cream cheese and 100ml milk until smooth, then add 3 eggs, 100g grated parmesan cheese, and season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  3. 3.3. Prick the crust bottom with a fork, pour in the filling, and bake at 175 degrees C for 25 minutes, covering with foil after 12.5 minutes.
  4. 4.4. Slice 350g mini tomatoes and combine with 3tbsp olive oil, 3tbsp white vinegar, 1 tbsp honey, salt, and pepper in a pan.
  5. 5.5. Place the tomato slices on the tart, avoiding excess liquid, and decorate with basil leaves.
